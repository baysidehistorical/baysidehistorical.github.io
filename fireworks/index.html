Independence Day Celebration at Fort Totten Park

Bayside Historical Society is hosting an Independence Day Celebration with a free concert and fireworks display by Grucci in Fort Totten Park sponsored by Councilmember Paul A. Vallone.

Event Schedule for July 1, 2015

Musical Performances MM Rockband Camp 6 - 7 PM
Phil Costa & Something Special Band 7 - 9 PM
Fireworks Display On the parade grounds of the park 9:15 PM

Bring your blankets, lawn chairs, and picnics for an evening of community fun. Vending trucks will be on site with a variety of foods, snacks, and beverages including roasted corn and fresh lemonade, funnel cakes, and corn dogs.
This is a non-alcohol event! All bags, coolers, backpacks, etc. will be inspected before entering the park. Limited parking will be available outside of Fort Totten in designated lots and streets. Pedaling, walking, swimming, skating, or busing to the event is encouraged!
