College Level and Adult Tours

On View

BHS offers guided tours of the Castle and its exhibitions for groups of up to 20 participants. Tours are approximately 45 minutes long; $6 per person with a minimum of five people. Guided tours are available during the week between 10am and 2pm.

At the Cemetery

BHS offers guided tours of the Lawrence Cemetery in Bayside for groups up to 40 participants. Tours are approximately 1 hour long. $6 per person with a minimum of ten people. Please note that there is no seating or restrooms at the cemetery.
