The Castle has a wheelchair accessible ramp with first floor access and a wheelchair accessible restroom. There is no elevator to the second floor, but there is a chair stair lift in place.

Directions:

The entrance to Fort Totten is located at the corner of Bell Boulevard and 212th Street.

Car: Clearview Expressway to Exit 7 (Willets Point Boulevard); turn right onto Bell Boulevard; turn left onto 212th Street which leads into Fort Totten - or - Cross Island Parkway North to Exit 32 (Bell Boulevard) and make a right at the light into Little Bay Park and Fort Totten Park.

Arrangements to drive into the park must be made in advance. Please call the Castle for details. 718.352.1548

Bus: Q13 or Q16 to Fort Totten Park.

LIRR: Port Washington Line to Bayside, then either cab or bus to Fort Totten Park.
